Why We Do This and How It Works

Most individuals, creators, and businesses dont need a full technical team only dependable help when something stops working. Thats why we started Green Ocean Consultants: to make technical support simple, quick, and easy to access.

We help companies that:

  • dont have a technical person on staff
  • struggle with WordPress, Cloudflare, or DNS setup
  • lose customers due to website errors
  • waste time trying to fix issues themselves

How it works is simple:
